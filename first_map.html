Gun Shops in Anchorage, Alaska

Alaska is the leading state for the largest number in gun ownership in America. And unfortunately, it is also the leading state in the number of deaths by guns. The map displays the top highly rated--four stars and above-- gun shops all within a 5-20 mile radius in Alaska's most populated city. An interesting fact to consider is Alaska's gun laws do not require a person of age to hold a gun permit.
